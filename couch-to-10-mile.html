Couch to 10 Miles

A beginner-friendly run/walk progression for nonexperienced runners who want to build toward the same 10-mile goal week. Repeat weeks as needed if the jump feels too aggressive.

Designed for true beginners

The early weeks use run/walk intervals, then gradually shift toward the same long-run outcome as the main plan.

Important!
