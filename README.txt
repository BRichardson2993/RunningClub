Upload the whole folder to your website and open index.html.

Notes:
- The main plan uses the uploaded spreadsheet and compresses it by starting at the original Week 3 row, giving 20 weeks from July 6, 2026 through November 22, 2026.
- The long run appears as Tuesday group-run option or Sunday long-run option. Runners should choose one long run per week, not both.
