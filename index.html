Thanks for joining the group!

Our original idea was to get a group of people together regardless of skill level and work toward a common goal - a loop around Lake Hefner in the fall. Some of us have been running for years, some of us have never ran, and some of us are walkers. It's all good here. We're here to build each other up. If you want to join us in that 10 mile goal then come on! If you think that sounds hideous but love getting together for some casual exercise and friendship then you are equally welcome! The only rule we have here is be cool and be kind. #onelove
